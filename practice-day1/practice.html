Troy: Fall of a City is a historical drama based on the legends of ancient Greece. The series tells the story of the conflict between the kingdoms of Troy and Greece, which begins when Prince Paris of Troy falls in love with Helen, the wife of a Greek king. Their relationship triggers a long and devastating war, while the series explores themes of love, honor, betrayal, and the struggle for power.
